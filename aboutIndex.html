About Tricheco Pizza

     Tricheco Pizza is a family-owned business that has been around since the year 1104. The first-ever location was opened in Nuuk Greenland by great-great-great-great-great-great-great-great grand poppa Tricheco Re. Our signature pizza the Big Poppa encapsulates everything it means to be a Greenland pizza chief with a combination of 3 types of fish, 2 types of shellfish, and a garnish of seaweed. We offer delivery services and are open 24-7-364 (Closed on June 21st for National Greenland Day).
